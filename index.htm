Craven Hill Gardens edges the Bayswater, Paddington and Lancaster Gate areas within the City of Westminster, London, England. It consists primarily of hotels, residences and gardens including landmarks such as The Hempel and Corringham.

Nearby attractions and services include Paddington Station (with the Heathrow Express), Queensway, Whiteleys, Bayswater Road, Connaught Village, Westbourne Grove and Hyde Park. The area is within Transport for London's Zone 1 and near to the London Underground stations of Paddington, Bayswater, Queensway and Lancaster Gate. Connecting streets are Leinster Terrace, Leinster Gardens and Craven Hill, all within the London Congestion Charge Zone.

For those living in the area, or just visiting, this site provides information on travel, sights and history.
